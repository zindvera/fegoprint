Cookies Policy

This Cookies Policy explains how FegoPrint uses cookies or similar storage when you visit the website.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that a website can store on your device. They are often used to remember preferences or improve how a site works.

2. How FegoPrint uses cookies

FegoPrint uses only minimal cookies or similar browser storage. The main purpose is to improve the preview experience and avoid showing the same message too many times.

  • A small cookie or local storage entry may be used to remember whether you have already seen a camera preview popup, so the same message does not keep appearing on every visit.
  • This information is limited to that setting and is not used to track you across other websites.

3. No advertising or tracking cookies

FegoPrint does not use cookies for advertising, cross‑site tracking or building marketing profiles.

4. Managing cookies

You can control or delete cookies in your browser settings.

  • You can clear existing cookies or site data at any time.
  • You can choose to block cookies, but some small convenience features (such as remembering that a popup has already been shown) may no longer work as expected.

5. Third‑party websites

When you click a “Buy” button or other link and go to an external store such as Amazon or Redbubble, that website may use its own cookies.

Their cookies are controlled by their own policies, not by FegoPrint.

6. Updates to this policy

This Cookies Policy may be updated from time to time. When the text on this page changes, the updated policy applies from the date it is published here.
